Information Collection and Use

For a better experience, while using our Service, we require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, specifically the images you choose to upload for analysis. The information that we request will be retained by us and used as described in this privacy policy.

The core functionality of FaceScore Pro involves analyzing facial features from images you upload. When you upload an image:

  • The image is securely transmitted to a third-party Artificial Intelligence (AI) service provider specifically Grok AI for processing.
  • The AI service analyzes the facial features within the image.
  • The resulting analysis (face score/analysis data) is returned to the app and displayed to you.

Image Data Handling:

  • We require your explicit action to upload an image.
  • Uploaded images are used *solely* for the purpose of providing the face analysis feature through our AI partner.
  • We do not store your uploaded images on our servers after the analysis is complete and the results have been returned to the app. The processing is intended to be ephemeral on our side.
  • The analysis results generated from the image are displayed within the app for your viewing and are not persistently stored on our servers.
